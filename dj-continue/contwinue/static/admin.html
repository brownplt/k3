Submission Categories

Specify the different kinds of submissions that there are. If there is more than one, authors will indicate which category their submission is in, and whether they want it to be considered for other categories as well. Categories may be different "tracks", or they may be different kinds of presentations, like "Paper", "Poster", etc. Some conferences may have only one category, "Accepted".

Other Decisions

If you want to be able to set papers' decision status to something other than Undecided, Rejected, or one of the categories above, you may add additional categories here. This may be a temporary status, like "consider for poster" or "decide by June 8th", or any kind of decision other than the categories above. These will not be visible to authors in any way.

Submission Components

Specify what components you want applicants to submit below. Text components, like abstracts, will be displayed directly within Continue; other components must be downloaded."Max" specifies a maximum file size in MB. Setting a maximum file size of 0 will allow unlimited-size uploads; maximum size don't apply to text components. If you check the box in the "Man" column, the component will be marked as mandatory.

Topics

Specify what topics authors can indicate in their submissions.

Email PC Members or Submitters

Use this page to send email and reviews to submitters or PC members, or to export their email addresses as a text file. You can send messages to the PC, to all the submitters at once, or to any decision category individually.

These emails will come from the "Designated Contact Admin", which may be changed in the "Users" tab of the Admin page.
